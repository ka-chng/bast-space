About Us

Welcome to Bast Space, where coffee meets compassion. Founded in 2020 by Abdulrahman Jadallah, a veterinarian dedicated to animal welfare, Bast Space was created to address the growing issue of stray cat overpopulation in Jordan. Combining veterinary expertise with a passion for creating welcoming spaces, Bast Space provides a sanctuary for rescued cats while offering visitors a unique experience

At Bast Space

You can relax in a cozy environment, enjoy a high-quality drink included in your entrance fee of 3 JDs, and spend time with our adoptable cats. Every visit directly supports the medical care, vaccinations, treatments, and neutering of these rescued animals.

Adoption

With our veterinary knowledge and commitment to animal care, we ensure that every cat at Bast Space receives the highest level of attention and medical support. Our mission is not only to provide a safe temporary home but also to help each cat find a loving, permanent family through adoption
